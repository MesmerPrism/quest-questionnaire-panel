Hello and welcome. Thank you for being here. You are already doing very well. There is nothing you need to prove or achieve; simply being here is enough. You may take all the time you need. If at any point you want to, you may press the big red button, but only if you want to. There is no pressure, and you never have to press it. Doing nothing, sitting quietly, looking around, and breathing are all valid choices.

The button is very big, noticeably big, almost surprisingly big, larger than a button needs to be, and that is part of its charm. It has presence and patience. It is red, deeply and undeniably red, the kind of red that knows what it is. From time to time it may blink gently, not to rush or pressure you, only to remind you that it is there. It is big, red, available, and occasionally blinking, as if saying hello, I am the big red button.

You may press it now, later, or not at all. There is no wrong way to be here; there is only your way. Take your time, look at the button or do not, think about pressing it or do not, wait for a blink or do not. If you want to press it, press it; if you do not, sit peacefully, gently, perfectly, while the button continues being big, red, and occasionally blinking. In your own time, it is entirely up to you.
